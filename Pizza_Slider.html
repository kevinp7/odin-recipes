Pizza Slider

These sliders really taste like pizza (as they should) but whit a hint of sweetness from the Hawaiian rolls. Trying to separate them can get messy but they are absolutely delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 cup finely pre-shredded italian cheese blend
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 teaspoon dried italian seasoning, divided
  • 2 tablespoons sun-dried tomato pesto

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Stir together mozzarella, Italian cheese blend, basil, pepper, and 1 1/2 teaspoons of the Italian seasoning in a small bowl until combined. Set aside.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese melts, about 15 minutes.
