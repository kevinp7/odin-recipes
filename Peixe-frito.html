Tilapia Fillets

Tilapia Fillets using simple seasoning and fast cooking

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil cooking spray
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpouse flour
  • 1 teaspoons seafood seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • half teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 pound tilapia fillets
  • 4 lemon slices

Instructions:

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F
  2. Whisk flour, seafood seasoning, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl.
  3. Lightly spray filets with olive oil, and sprinkle flour seasoning over each filet
  4. Cook until tilapia filets flake easily with a fork, no need to flip them up
